General Faq

What kind of stuff do you post here?

Short answer? Cool stuff you can actually use. Long answer? Tips on tech, freelancing, sleep, psychology, browsers, gear… and occasionally, unhinged Gen Z humor.

Who runs this blog?

Just one guy — vibing, thinking, writing, and trying to turn curiosity into content. (And hopefully, cash. Let’s be honest.)

Are these guides legit? Where do you get info from?

Most stuff is researched through a combo of personal experience, forums, tech blogs, psychology books, and sometimes just trial and error. We don’t pretend to know everything — we just try to explain it better.

Can I suggest a topic or ask something?

Absolutely. Hit the contact page or drop a comment. Weird questions are welcome. The weirder, the better.

Will there be more tech-related content?

Oh yeah. Expect posts about gear (mouse, keyboard, monitor specs), software tools, AI tools, and how-to guides for building sites, freelancing, and more.

Do you have social media or a newsletter?

Not yet, but it's coming. Right now it’s just raw blog content — no fluff. Once we build a bit more, we’ll roll out the fun stuff.
